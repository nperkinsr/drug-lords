Instructions

Welcome to Drug Lords! Your goal is to make as much money as possible by buying low and selling high.

How to Play:

➤ You start with $700 in your pocket.

➤ Travel around Smuggleston to buy and sell drugs at different prices.

➤ Prices change daily—keep an eye on the market for the best deals.

➤ Your goal is to have at least $10,000 by the end of Day 30.

➤ You must pay off your debt before the game ends, or you lose.

➤ Carrying more than 15 units of drugs gives you a 50% chance of getting busted by the cops.

➤ If the cops bust you, they will confiscate all your drugs and fine you half of your money.

➤ Check the news daily for important updates that might affect your deals.
